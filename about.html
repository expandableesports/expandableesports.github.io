About Us

ExPandable is a multimedia esports organization that provides a platform for content creators to connect and gain access to helpful resources.

We also maintain a community aimed to bring together people to support one another. From content creators to digital media artists, we are creating a framework to streamline the process of maintaining individual brand growth.
